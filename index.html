How It Works

From seismic data
to discovery decision

Four autonomous steps that take your team from raw seismic volumes to confident trap predictions — without leaving the platform.

🗺 STEP 01

Navigate the Subsurface

Move through seismic volumes layer by layer — like Google Maps, but for the earth. Each horizon is a navigable, interactive surface.

🔍 STEP 02

Detect Anomalies

AI agents continuously scan horizon layers and automatically flag structural anomalies — bulges, amplitude variations, impedance contrasts — the moment they appear.

🧊 STEP 03

Reconstruct in 3D

Detected anomalies are instantly rebuilt in three dimensions using your SEG-Y data and formation metadata, giving you immediate spatial clarity.

🎯 STEP 04

Predict the Trap

Receive a confidence-scored trap prediction expressed in the depth, amplitude, and impedance parameters your team already uses — grounded, explainable, actionable.
